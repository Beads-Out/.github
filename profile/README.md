# Beads Out Walkthrough

Welcome to the official GitHub organization for [BeadsOut.org](https://beadsout.org)!

## About Us

We are an independent companion site for [Beads Out](https://beadsout.org), the ASMR color-sorting puzzle game published by **VOODOO**. The game looks calm, then turns nasty fast. Beads move along a conveyor, boxes fill up, and one bad choice can jam the whole board.

We built this site for players who are stuck on hard stages, weird mechanics, or late-game bottlenecks. Instead of wasting boosters or retries, you can jump straight to the exact level guide you need.

Our mission:

- **Full Level Walkthroughs:** Clean, level-by-level solutions.
- **Fast Answers:** Open the exact stage and get unstuck.
- **Mechanic Breakdowns:** Help for **Mystery Boxes**, **Multicolor Crates**, **Crate Locks**, **Shape Sorts**, **Half Boxes**, and more.
- **Booster-Saving Strategy:** Practical advice so you do not burn premium tools on easy boards.

> Note: We are an independent fan site created for informational purposes only. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to **VOODOO**.

## Quick Tips

If you want to clear a level without opening the answer first, do these checks:

1. **Conveyor check:** Do not let the belt get too full. Most losses come from jams, not slow tapping.
2. **Lock check:** If the level has a key box or lock chain, clear that first.
3. **Sequence check:** On **Multicolor Crates**, the order matters. One wrong bead can wreck the whole run.
4. **Mystery check:** Keep spare room open when a hidden box is on the board.
5. **Half Box check:** Merge incomplete tubs early before the main bead flow gets ugly.
6. **Booster check:** Save **+1 Extra Slot**, **Move Box**, and **Fill A Box** for real bottlenecks.
